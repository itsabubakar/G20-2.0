Celery

abg Africa Systems Ttd owns and operates the 1st Commercial Hydroponics farm in Sub-Saharan Africa located on its property along Unguwan Muazu expressway Kaduna. The commercial hydroponics farm is fully automated. It is top-of-the-range EU standard NFT system with a capacity to harvest 10,000 heads of vegetables weekly.
